WebPad

This is an Angular application.

Tools used: Angular CLI, Angular Material, Bootstrap

Its same as an online notepad where you can quickly note your to-do tasks and accomplish them one by one without forgetting any of them.

You can write whatever you want in the 'Add your notes' part and press the 'add' button or press 'enter' on your keyboard to enter the notes. You can then remove them individually using the 'delete' button or remove them all at once using the 'Remove All' button.
